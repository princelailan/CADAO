About CADAO

Our Mission

To empower a generation of highly skilled youth, promote livelihoods through agriculture, and enhance climate change mitigation practices in rural communities of Kenya.

Our Vision

Creating sustainable development through agriculture, youth empowerment, and environmental conservation in Homabay County.

Our History

Founded in 2021, CADAO was established to address the challenges faced by rural communities in Homabay County through sustainable development initiatives.
